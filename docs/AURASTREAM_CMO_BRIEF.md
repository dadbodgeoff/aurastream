## PRODUCT OVERVIEW

### What is AuraStream?

AuraStream is an AI-powered creative asset generation platform designed specifically for content creators, streamers, and influencers. It's a "Streamer Studio SaaS" that enables creators to generate professional-quality branded assets in seconds without design skills.

### Core Value Proposition

**"Professional streaming assets in seconds, not hours."**

AuraStream eliminates the need for expensive designers or complex tools like Photoshop. Creators describe what they want, and AI generates on-brand assets instantly.

### Target Audience (TAM/SAM/SOM)

**Primary ICP (Ideal Customer Profile):**
- Twitch streamers (affiliate to partner level)
- YouTube content creators (10K-500K subscribers)
- TikTok creators building cross-platform presence
- Emerging influencers investing in personal brand

**Demographics:**
- Age: 18-35
- Gender: Skews male (65/35) but female creator segment growing fast
- Income: $30K-$100K (mix of full-time creators and side-hustlers)
- Tech-savvy, mobile-first, value speed and aesthetics

**Psychographics:**
- Ambitious, growth-minded
- Time-poor (streaming + editing + community management)
- Brand-conscious but not design-skilled
- Price-sensitive but willing to pay for tools that save time
- Community-driven, influenced by peer recommendations

### Competitive Landscape

**Direct Competitors:**
- Canva (general design, not creator-focused)
- Placeit (templates, limited AI)
- StreamElements/Streamlabs (overlays only)
- Fiverr/99designs (expensive, slow turnaround)

**AuraStream Differentiators:**
1. AI-native (not template-based)
2. Creator-specific asset types (emotes, panels, alerts, etc.)
3. Brand kit system maintains consistency
4. "Vibe Branding" - upload inspiration, AI extracts your style
5. Speed: assets in seconds vs. days

## PRODUCT FEATURES (Marketing Angles)

### 1. Vibe Branding™
Upload images that inspire you → AI extracts colors, mood, style → Creates your brand DNA automatically.

**Marketing angle:** "Your vibe, instantly captured. No design degree required."

### 2. Quick Create Templates
Pre-built templates for common creator needs:
- Going Live announcements
- Milestone celebrations (1K followers, etc.)
- Offline screens
- Starting Soon screens
- Emotes
- Clip highlights

**Marketing angle:** "One click. Done. Back to creating content."

### 3. Profile Creator (PFP Generator)
AI-generated profile pictures and avatars in your brand style.

**Marketing angle:** "Stand out in every comment section."

### 4. Aura Lab (Experimental)
Fusion-style creative experimentation - combine elements to discover new styles.

**Marketing angle:** "Your creative playground. No rules."

### 5. Brand Kit System
Save colors, fonts, logos, voice/tone → Every asset stays on-brand automatically.

**Marketing angle:** "Consistency without the spreadsheet."

### 6. Prompt Coach (Studio Tier)
AI assistant that helps craft better prompts for better results.

**Marketing angle:** "Like having a creative director in your pocket."
